Welcome!

Hi! I'm Avani :) I recently graduated from Harvey Mudd College with a BS in Physics and Computer Science. I am interested in the intersection of machine learning, data, modelling, physics, and science, especially in climate and energy systems. During my undergrad, I worked in the Gerbode Lab for Soft Matter Physics under Professor Sharon Gerbode and in the FICUS Lab (a computational climate lab) under Professor Sarah Kavassalis. I've also worked on my capstone project with LS Power and been a Software Engineering Intern at SpaceX. I'm currently working as an Analyst at Aurora Energy Research and on the Machine Learning Team at ExploraVist, an early-stage startup developing AI-powered glasses. I'm enthusiastic for bringing my passion for social impact into my future interdisciplinary work.

Outside of work, I love being outside, singing, reading, both journaling & journalism, and telling anti-jokes.
